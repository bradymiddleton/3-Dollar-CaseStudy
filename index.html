03 — Historical Context

Markets reward patience

The 7% real return assumption isn't optimism — it's history. S&P 500 real returns have averaged ~7% annually since 1926, rewarding investors who stayed the course through every crash, recession, and panic.

S&P 500 — Historical Real Returns by Decade
1990–2000 Tech Boom — bull market despite 1990 recession +14.2% real avg.
2000–2010 Dot-com crash + 2008 GFC — "The Lost Decade" -0.9% real avg.
2010–2020 Post-crisis recovery through COVID shock +11.2% real avg.
1970–1980 Stagflation era — high inflation, volatile markets -1.4% real avg.
1980–1990 Reagan bull market, S&L crisis +10.4% real avg.
1926–2024 Long-run average — all cycles included ~7.0% real

04 — Financial Optionality

The Bridge to Leverage

Capital accumulation is not the end goal — it's the key. Once Maya's portfolio crosses key thresholds, it unlocks access to wealth-building vehicles that were previously inaccessible without inherited capital.

🏠
Investment Property Down Payment
Unlocks at Year 15 (~$35k)
A $35k down payment on a $175k rental property (20%) in a secondary market generates monthly cash flow and appreciating equity — a second income stream built entirely from the pivot.
Est. rental yield: 6–8% / yr · Appreciation: 3–4% / yr
🛡️
Indexed Universal Life (IUL) Seed
Unlocks at Year 5 (~$8k)
An IUL policy funded with lump-sum seed capital creates a tax-advantaged death benefit and cash value tied to index performance — the rich person's tool, now accessible to Maya through discipline.
Cash value growth: 5–10% (floor protection) · Tax-free withdrawals in retirement
🏢
Business Line of Credit Collateral
Unlocks at Year 20 (~$55k)
A brokerage account with $55k+ can serve as collateral for a SBLOC (Securities-Backed Line of Credit), giving Maya access to $30–45k in low-interest capital to launch a business without liquidating her position.
SBLOC rate: Prime + 1–2% · No liquidation required
🎓
529 College Fund / Generational Transfer
Unlocks at Any Milestone
At any threshold, Maya can redirect contributions into a 529 plan for a child or redirect gains into a custodial brokerage account — seeding the next generation's financial foundation from scratch.
529 avg. growth: 6–8% · Federal tax-free for qualified education

Project Notes

Created for a data visualization course final project exploring micro-investment behavioral finance.

The thesis: "strategic redirection" — not frugality — of $3/day unlocks financial optionality for young adults (ages 18–33) without inherited capital. Visualizations built with D3.js v7.
